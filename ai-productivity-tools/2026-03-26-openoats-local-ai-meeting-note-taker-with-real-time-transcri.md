# OpenOats: Local AI Meeting Note-Taker with Real-Time Transcription and Knowledge Base Integration

## Summary
OpenOats is an open-source macOS application that functions as a real-time meeting note-taker. It transcribes both sides of a conversation locally and privately, then leverages an integrated knowledge base of your personal notes to surface relevant information during the call. This tool is designed for privacy-conscious individuals aiming to enhance their meeting performance by providing contextual information without any audio leaving their device.

## Key Insights
- OpenOats operates entirely locally on a Mac, performing offline transcription and, when paired with Ollama, running all LLM suggestions and embeddings without any network calls, ensuring maximum privacy.
- It offers real-time transcription and a unique 'knowledge base search' feature that sifts through the user's Markdown or plain text files to present relevant talking points precisely when they are needed during a conversation.
- The application provides flexibility in AI model choice, allowing users to select between cloud providers via OpenRouter (e.g., GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini) or local models like Llama via Ollama for AI-powered suggestions.
- The OpenOats app window remains hidden by default from screen sharing, making its use discreet and non-intrusive to other meeting participants.
