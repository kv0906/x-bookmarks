# Crypto Tokenization & DeFi Infrastructure

The convergence of traditional finance and decentralized finance through asset tokenization — turning real-world assets into blockchain tokens with transparent ownership, automated risk management, and cross-chain interoperability. Practical security of DeFi wallets remains a foundational concern.

## Key Points
- **tbDEX** (Square/Block, Jack Dorsey): decentralized protocol for exchanging Bitcoin, fiat, and real-world assets using decentralized identity (DID) and verifiable credentials (VCs) for trust — bridging TradFi and DeFi without centralized intermediaries
- **Chainlink** as critical infrastructure: Data Feeds, CCIP (Cross-Chain Interoperability Protocol), and Proof of Reserve solve the three core challenges of tokenization — data/compliance enrichment, cross-chain liquidity, and cross-chain synchronization
- **Tokenization challenges**: enriching assets with real-world data, securing connectivity across multi-chain ecosystems, keeping data consistent as assets move between chains
- **DeFi wallet security**: practical checklist covering wallet selection, 2FA, hardware wallet best practices, and continuous monitoring — addressing the common vulnerabilities (weak passwords, neglected 2FA, over-reliance on hot wallets) that account for significant crypto losses
